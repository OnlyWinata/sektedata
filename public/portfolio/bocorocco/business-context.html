Sebelum menganalisis data, pahami dulu bisnisnya — siapa mereka, apa yang dijual, dan bagaimana model distribusi premium footwear di Indonesia.

Bocorocco adalah brand premium footwear Indonesia-Italia yang diproduksi oleh PT Chosen Mitra Abadi. Mengusung Pillow Concept Technology™ — insole 9 lapis yang dipatenkan secara global — Bocorocco memposisikan diri di segmen super premium dengan harga Rp 3,59 juta hingga Rp 22,39 juta per pasang.

Pillow Concept Technology™

Bocorocco's core differentiator is the world-patented 9-layer insole technology that provides superior comfort and pain reduction. This technology is the foundation of the brand's premium positioning and is central to their marketing claims.

🏅 Key Certifications & Partnerships

Bocorocco received Halal certification in August 2024 and became the Official Footwear Partner of PSSI/Timnas Indonesia — strengthening brand credibility in both religious and sports markets.

Competitive Landscape

Bocorocco competes in the premium comfort footwear segment against established international brands:

  • Hush Puppies — comfort casual, mid-premium positioning
  • Geox — breathable technology, Italian heritage
  • Ecco — Scandinavian comfort, direct injection technology
  • Clarks — British heritage, comfort-focused
  • Brodo — Indonesian premium brand, younger demographic
