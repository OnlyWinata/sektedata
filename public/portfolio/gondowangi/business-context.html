Portfolio · 01

PT Gondowangi Tradisional Kosmetika

Before analyzing data, understand the business first — who they are, what they sell, and what challenges they face in the Indonesian FMCG market.

Gondowangi is an Indonesian FMCG company specializing in personal care products. They operate 4 brands across 8 product categories, competing head-to-head with multinational giants like Unilever and P&G.

Competitive Landscape

Gondowangi operates in one of the most competitive FMCG markets in Southeast Asia. Their competitors include:

  • Unilever — Dove, Sunsilk, Clear, TRESemmé, Lifebuoy, Lux (dominant in hair & body care)
  • P&G — Pantene, Head & Shoulders, Rejoice (strong in shampoo)
  • Paragon (local) — Wardah, Make Over, Emina (dominant in halal cosmetics)
  • L'Oréal Group — Maybelline, L'Oréal Paris, Garnier (premium positioning)

Core Business Challenges

  1. Low market share (9.4%) — competing against multinationals with massive marketing budgets
  2. Brand visibility — Natur and Azalea are known but not top-of-mind in their categories
  3. Regional gaps — strong in Java, weak in Eastern Indonesia
  4. Channel optimization — e-commerce growing fast but margin pressure from platform fees
